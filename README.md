# Autoscreenshoterpy

...is a python gui for taking manually or automatically screenshots of a selected frame of your screen.

After clicking on "Positions" you select your frame by choosing the top-left and then the bottom-right corner of your frame with your mouse.  

The position is saved and you can take screenshots manually or let the programm take screenshots automatically by detecting changes on the screen.

You can also export all images to a single `.pdf` document.

Here is an <a href="https://github.com/jk31/autoscreenshoterpy/raw/master/example.pdf">example document</a> of a <a href="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=FgakZw6K1QQ">Video</a>.
